Bruschetta

by Kathy Bias

Bruschetta is a classic Italian appetizer that is easy to make at home. Toasted bread is topped with tomatoes, Parmesan cheese, garlic, and fresh basil. Use a high-quality balsamic vinegar for best results.

Ingredients

  • French bread - 1 loaf (cut in 1/4-inch slices)
  • Extra virgin olive oil - 1 tablespoon
  • Roma tomatoes - 8 (diced)
  • Fresh basil - 0.33 cup
  • Freshly grated Parmigiano cheese - 1 ounce
  • Garlic cloves - 2 (minced)
  • Balsamic Vinegar - 1 tablespoon
  • Kosher salt - 0.25 teaspoon
  • Freshly ground black pepper - 0.25 teaspoon

Preparation steps

  1. Get your bread crispy and toasty

    Preheat oven to 400 degrees F (200 degrees C). Brush bread slices on both sides lightly with 1 tablespoon oil and place on large baking sheet. Toast bread until golden, 5 to 10 minutes, turning halfway through.

  2. Prepare the tomato mixture

    Meanwhile, toss together tomatoes, basil, Parmesan cheese, and garlic in a bowl. Mix in balsamic vinegar, 2 teaspoons olive oil, kosher salt, and pepper.

  3. Serve and enjoy

    Spoon tomato mixture onto toasted bread slices and serve immediately.
